DescriptionSoftware Freedom Day (SFD) is an annual worldwide celebration of Free Software. SFD is a public education effort with the aim of increasing awareness of Free Software and its virtues, and encouraging its use. For the fourth consecutive year, this event will be organized in Casablanca, Morocco.
