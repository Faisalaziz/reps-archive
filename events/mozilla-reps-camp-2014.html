Organized byRosana Ardila
Start2014-09-12T07:00:00Z
End2014-09-14T18:00:00Z
TimezoneEurope/Berlin
DescriptionLeadership of the Mozilla Reps program, comprised of Council members, Peers and Reps mentors, will meet for 3 days in Berlin, Germany, from September 12th to September 14st, 2014. The meeting will be comprised of presentations, breakout sessions and discussions to draft the 2014/2015 Mozilla Reps program roadmap. This will be the second time the entire program leadership meet and work together in person.
