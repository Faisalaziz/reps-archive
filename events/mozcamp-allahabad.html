DescriptionThe idea is to organise a 2day Mozilla camp at Allahabad that will introduce HTML5 to local students. We will conduct workshops on making HTML5 web apps and HTML5 mobile apps over the two day period, and also guide students towards open source and summer of code. SCHEDULE Day 1: HTML5 Web apps workshop Day 2: HTML5 Mobile apps workshop Open house discussion on “FOSS & Students” Quiz & Prizes This will help create a local community in a technologically-remote area.
