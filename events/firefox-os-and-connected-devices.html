DescriptionIn December 2015 Mozilla announced that it's focus on Firefox OS will pivot into the direction of Connected Devices (Web of Things). Imagine what could be done with an open web platform on small devices, connected to each other. Firefox OS on your fridge? Who knows? Michael will show the latest development and experiments that Mozilla has been working on.
