DescriptionThis campaign is divided into 2 parts: 1- Online campaign is to provide a privacy tip a day that an average user could complete in 10 minutes or less. Tips will be shared through social media, such as Facebook and Twitter. 2- Offline Event aims to make users and makers of the web care, learn, teach and share about Online Privacy. Also we want to empower the users of the web to protect their privacy and control their digital footprint.
