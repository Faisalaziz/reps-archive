DescriptionFebruary 28th is celebrated throughout India as National Science Day. It celebrated by organising one of the biggest science festivals in the country on Feb 28th and the next day at the GMRT campus in Khodad. This festival sees about 20,000 people visiting the festival every year. Mozilla has been participating in the National Science Day Festival from the past 3 years. The main goal for participating in this festival has been creating awareness among people about open web.
