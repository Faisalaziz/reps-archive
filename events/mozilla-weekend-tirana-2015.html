DescriptionThis is the second edition of the event inspired by the sucess of the first Mozilla Weekend in February 2014. Mozilla Weekend aims to bring the community closer together by working, socializing and sharing ideas. It also aims into bringing interested people into the community and showing them what Mozilla is about, in order to grow the local community even more It will be held in a well located university in Tirana, for 2 days, 5 hours a day.
