DescriptionThe Creativity Workshop Web Mozilla Webmaker will be held on 10th and 12th of June at 14h, the event's goal is to promote and stimulate the development of the web through web programming activities and promote free software, specifically the Mozilla Firefox browser . The event will take place at DAS NOROESTE Campinas (Rua Salvador Allende, S / N, Jardim Nova Esperança, Campinas / São Paulo), the target audience is young people aged 12 to 28 years.
