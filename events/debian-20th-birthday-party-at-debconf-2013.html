DescriptionDebian is turning 20 this year and Debian invites everyone to the 20th Debian Birthday Party at DebConf 2013. This year the DebConf will be in Vaumarcus, Switzerland. In the afternoon there will be talks and in the evening there will be a barbecue and opportunity to talk to Debian people. Please register at https://wiki.debconf.org/wiki/DebConf13/BirthdayPartyRegistration , registration for barbecue is mandatory.
