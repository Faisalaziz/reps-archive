DescriptionAs part of a pilot project for Mozilla l10n, we will be holding a l10n sprint at FOSDEM with any localizer who is interested. We will be setting goals for the next months for the various communities and inspect what can be improved. This will be a very flexible event and people can jump in and out during the day. As a little thank you there will be badges and beers for every localizer who took part in the event!
