DescriptionThis event has the objective of advancing the conformation and empowerment of localization tools and processes for communities speaking the Shuar and Quichua languages. In this event it is announced aspects on the development, use and dissemination of Free Software, and the advantages of its implementation for the technological progress of society. There will be talks, workshops, tournaments and a free technology hackathon focused on l10n.
