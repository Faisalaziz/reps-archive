DescriptionThis is a slightly different MozCoffee in Thessaloniki, Greece. This time our local mozilla contributors are going to run four presentations on the latest technologies such as the status of Firefox OS, Firefox Developer Edition, WebIDE, Mozilla stumbler and Firefox l10n. After the presentations we are going to run 4 different hands-on stations for each topic / technology. Our main focus is to engage more contributors from the local community.
