DescriptionFuturo Móvil 2012 is an event organized by the Free Software Community and the Government of Bolivia, which aims to show the importance of mobile technology for the future of the country, and which is intended to teach workshops, languages ​​and platforms for mobile applications . Mozilla Bolivia has been invited to show the advantages of Firefox OS and give a workshop about developing applications for OS Firefox.
