TimezoneEurope/Berlin
DescriptionThe Mozilla Policy Weekend is about shaping the community agenda of Mozilla in Germany for policy related activities. It is a mini-conference with a mostly internal focus. It will also serve as an opportunity of policy team members to connect with local Mozillians. The main objective is to identify policy items for Germany and see how Mozilla participants from the local community could contribute in 2016.
