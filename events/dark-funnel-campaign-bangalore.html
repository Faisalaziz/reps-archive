DescriptionThe event will engage community members in understanding the safety concern of Firefox on the Internet. Community Members will use different combinations of keywords to find non-Mozilla sites that let users download Firefox without going www.mozilla.org first. Secondly, the community members will investigate these websites to find “unofficial Firefox download” buttons. And finally, the community members will document those sites.
