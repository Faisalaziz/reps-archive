DescriptionMozCamp Allahabad is an effort to build a Mozilla and FOSS community in northern state of the country (UP) starting with educational hub of the UP 'Allahabad'. Allahabad is also know as Prayag. One of the major objective of this event is to make people aware about the opportunities to get involve in Mozilla and FOSS initiatives. We have postponed the event date from 30th March to 20th April due to the unavailability of swag and HOLI.
