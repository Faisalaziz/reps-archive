Organized byJason Shultz

End2018-10-20T00:00:00Z
TimezoneAtlantic/Reykjavik
DescriptionIn 2018, the Icelandic locale community will meet together for the first time to discuss style guide, recruitment for the community and raise awareness of apps and programs available from Mozilla in the Icelandic language. Training and unifying of tools will also take place as community members exchange ideas and approaches they use in their localization process.
