DescriptionMaker Faire is a gathering of tech enthusiasts, crafters, educators, hobbyists, engineers, science clubs, authors, artists, students, and commercial exhibitors. All of these “makers” come to Maker Faire to show what they have made and to share what they have learned. Swedish Mozilla community plan to join the first ever mini maker faire in Stockholm. We plan to demo the upcoming Webmaker training and encourage people to participate, learn, share and possibly translate it to their own language.
