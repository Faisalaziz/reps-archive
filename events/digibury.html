DescriptionDigibury is the meet-up event for digital types in Kent! They meet up on Wednesday 2.0 (the second Wednesday in the month - oh hai!) in Canterbury for talks, demos, messing about with digital stuff and drinks. Around 50 people show up every month and they have 2 or 3 speakers giving talks which are short, sweet and personal with plenty of time for questions and conversation.
