Nairobi L10n meet up

Organized byStephen Wanjau
Start2013-05-18T08:00:00Z
End2013-05-18T13:00:00Z
TimezoneAfrica/Nairobi
DescriptionThe main aim of this meet up is to set up and rekindle the efforts of the Swahili L10n working group for Firefox browser for desktop. Other products L10n shall follow suit once we get the ball rolling. It is also a meet-up where new recruits who showed up at the App days event in January and Mozcofee in April shall be engaged and guided on how to go about L10n.
