Start2018-03-18T23:00:00Z

TimezoneEurope/Stockholm
DescriptionA pop up exhibition, learning space and lecture series revealing our online lives in an era of big data, privacy abuse and tech giants. The event features a Data Detox Bar with Data Detox Kits for visitors. The event runs in two libraries during All Digital Week, a campaign week for digital inclusion. Hosted by Stockholm Public Library in coop with Tactical Tech Collective (who invented the concept, with support from Mozilla), Young IT Coaches, Royal Institute of Technology and Inicio.
