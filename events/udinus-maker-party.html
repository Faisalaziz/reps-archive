DescriptionAn event from Dian Nuswantoro University (UDINUS) in Semarang, Indonesia. Through this event, we aim to celebrate the Freedom of Open Source Software (FOSS) and introduce the advantages of using FOSS to public, especially at campus. In line with that, we also teach the web to introduce the educational media from Mozilla Webmaker. The event is organized by UDINUS Firefox Club and Dinus Open Source Community, an open source community from the university.
