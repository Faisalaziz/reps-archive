DescriptionLinuxLive will showcase various Linux alternatives to Windows. Attendees will get to see the systems themselves, and many of the commonly used applications for everyday tasks. Switching to a new operating system can seem like a daunting task, as can switching to alternative applications. Members of the Bristol and Bath Linux User Group aim to show attendees just how easy it can be, and just how much more they can get out of using Linux on their PCs.
