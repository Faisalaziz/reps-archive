DescriptionThe Computer Science Summit (CSS) is a seminar-workshop for students from secondary and tertiary levels of education that encourages them to see Computer Science not only as a potential career but also as a means to help develop the country in the field of science and technology. On its 2nd year, CSS will again be gathering 200 students from top performing high schools & universities in Metro Manila to participate in a day-long conference at the College of Engineering, Melchor Hall, UP Diliman.
