Arabic Mozilla Meetup 2014

Organized byMohammed Migdadi
Start2014-10-31T07:00:00Z
End2014-11-03T10:00:00Z
TimezoneEurope/Istanbul
DescriptionDuring the Arabic Mozilla Meetup, 2014's version, our aim is to strengthen the inter-communities links and bring together active contributors from Arabic countries in one place for discussions and presentations mainly around localization and Firefox OS. We also intend to plan for the future of Mozilla's presence in the region, with the one overarching goal: getting a solid ground ready to welcome Firefox OS in the region.
