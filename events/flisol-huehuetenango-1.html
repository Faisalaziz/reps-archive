DescriptionFLISoL, an acronym for Latin American Free Software Installfest, is the biggest event for spreading the word about Free Software, and will held its 16th edition this year in more than 200 cities across Latin America. In Guatemala, the event will take place in Huehuetenango and it will have the participation of the Mozilla Guatemala community. Besides talking about Mozilla's mission, WoMoz, Common Voice and Privacy are some of the topics that will be covered at this event.
