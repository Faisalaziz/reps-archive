Description Software Freedom Day (SFD) is an annual worldwide celebration of Free Software. This year we are celebrating SFD on 15th September 2012. SFD is a public education effort with the aim of increasing awareness of Free Software and its virtues, and encouraging its use. As part of the event we will be spreading words of FOSS, Mozilla Projects and Drupal initiatives.
