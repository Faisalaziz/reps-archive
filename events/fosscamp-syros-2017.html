Organized byRedon Skikuli
Start2017-08-31T06:00:00Z
End2017-09-04T14:00:00Z
TimezoneEurope/Athens
DescriptionThe 2nd edition of the summer adventure to spend with free open source geeks and Mozillians from communities all around the Balkans and Europe. During this edition of FOSScamp we will focus on the theme: empowering the Commons in Syros Island through WebVR activities. We will shoot 360' photos of the island, publish them in Wikimedia commons and through a Mozilla WebVR workshops will learn how to create Web VR experiences with the images and at the same time contribute to Wikimedia Commons.
