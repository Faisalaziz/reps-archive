Organized bySan Emmanuel James
Start2014-07-11T06:00:00Z

DescriptionThe event is a huge 2 day maker party that will bring together educators, innovators, learners and makers from East Africa and beyond to share experiences and build the web together laying a foundation for growth of the internet and information of technology in Africa. This will be annual event that will later spread across Africa helping address the technological challenges faced in the African continent through meaningful collaborations.
