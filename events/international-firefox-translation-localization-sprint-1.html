DescriptionMozilla Utah will be putting on an international tranlation sprint for speakers of any language. The primary goal is to complete the es-MX Firefox desktop l10n work. We'll also focuss on other languages in Utah and have them translate their first strings or support articles. Those from Mozilla Utah will be one location and will work with l10n teams who have languages represented amongst us on IRC and Skype.
