Women's Webmaker Day

Organized byAlifiyah Ganijee
Start2014-03-15T05:00:00Z
End2014-03-15T10:00:00Z
TimezoneAfrica/Nairobi
DescriptionThis event aims to focus on the female participants within the community with the major objective of bringing on board more women participants as well to create further awareness on Women and Mozilla and the entire concept around it. During this event, attendants will use Mozilla's Webmaker tools to create an amazing web page or card that will convey a message based on problems facing women in Kenya and Africa at large.
