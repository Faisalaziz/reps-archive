DescriptionTo spread Localized Firefox in India, Mozilla India Localization community is hosting a pan-India campaign for Hindi & Marathi. The campaign would run from 2nd Oct to 1st Nov. We are creating unique, shareable pieces of content that are humorous, insightful and topical to get the conversation and activities started.
