DescriptionThe Free & Open Source Software Society of the University of the Aegean is organizing a series of lessons. This time we're going to present FirefoxOS, beginning with a short (~25mins) talk about FirefoxOS; then we'll show around the Keon phone so that students will have the opportunity to have a hands-on experience. Last but not least, we're going to have an open talk in order to co-ordinate regarding the local Firefox club in an attempt to establish a strong student community in Samos.
