DescriptionETESP is one of the most respected technical schools in São Paulo, and have the tradition in always let the students have access to the most new technologies in the market. The Firefox OS Hacking Days is a proposal to have 2 days with the computer students to talk about: - The history of Firefox OS, Firefox OS system structure, Webapps structure After a small presentation slides and a live webapp build, the students will code their own webapps using the Firefox OS Simulator.
