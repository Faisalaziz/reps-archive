DescriptionApp Day in a Box (ADIAB) Chile is a full or half day ‘hacking’ event for developers. The goal of the App Day is to introduce Firefox OS and Firefox Marketplace, and share knowledge about the tools available to help developers get started building apps to submit to the Marketplace. App Days can come in many sizes, depending on venue availability and size of community. You can use the tools and resources created for the first round of Firefox OS App Days.
