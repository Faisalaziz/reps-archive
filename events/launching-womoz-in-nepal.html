DescriptionAs this is the month of Women, we will be launching WoMoz properly in Nepal by gathering some interested female contributors and briefin them about Women empowerment in ICT and how they can contribute/help in the growing community of Mozilla. We will also have other general community discussions. Shristi, our active FSA and a WoMoz, will be working and hosting the event. She will be working closely with other Reps and FSAs + other experienced contributors for the planning of the event.
