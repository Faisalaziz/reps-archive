DescriptionTo honor the two year anniversary of the Mozilla Utah community we will be having an all-day celebration. Attendees will divide into two groups: App day - have a demo of a current FirefoxOS app, then divide into groups by app choice and start coding. Localization sprint - divide by teams and localize 750+ strings and finish two language packs: Navajo and Shoshone. We will conclude with a dinner and celebrate each attendee's impact from the days event.
