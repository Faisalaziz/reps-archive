DescriptionA gathering at ITS Rio to talk about "Makers and Digital Education" Participate in the conversation about the challenges posed to the diffusion of the maker culture, the role and potential of the Lan Houses. We will receive Carolina Althaller, from the Olabi Makerspace, and Anderson Balboni, owner of one of the first Lan Houses in communities in Rio de Janeiro. The proposal is to bring a variety of perspectives to deepen in this debate so important for social innovation in the country.
