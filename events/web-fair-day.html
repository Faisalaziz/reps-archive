DescriptionThis day is intended to raise awareness among young people for careers in Information Technology and Communication (ICT). Throughout the day, there will be presentations of success stories, workshops ... and a "news room" for live and online coverage. In the web Village, exhibitors will answer questions from visitors. Free software will be represented by a Linux local association and by Mozilla.
