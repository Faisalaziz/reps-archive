DescriptionThe Mozilla Central America community will participate in the 9th Central American Free Software Conference (ECSL) 2017 and in Mozilla Activate in Puntarenas, both events in Costa Rica. The volunteers of Mozilla Centroamérica have coordinated to participate in both events contemplating multiple activities in search of community growth in the region. The activities of the two events will be: talks, workshops and stands, which will be presented by Mozillians.
