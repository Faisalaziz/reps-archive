DescriptionCaipyra is an event inspired by Python Brazil (the largest Python event in Latin America) and aims, non-profit, to disseminate knowledge about various areas of technology. As the name says, Caipyra is a meeting organized by the Python community in the interior of São Paulo, for people interested in knowing or deepening in this language, but open to various technologies that are brought and approached by our distinguished guests.
