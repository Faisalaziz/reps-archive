DescriptionFLISOL is an event created to promote the free software general use, and create interactions between users and developers. FLISOL happens simultaneously in several countries of Latin America. Santos will host its eighth edition this year. In the event, free software are installed in a free way in the computers of participants who gets interested. Also are offered mini courses and talks on topics involving free software in all of its expressions: artistic, academic, business and social.
