MozillaPH Social Media Series: Women in Social Media

Organized byFaye Tandog
Start2014-03-22T05:00:00Z
End2014-03-22T09:00:00Z
TimezoneAsia/Manila
DescriptionIn celebration of the women's month, the Mozilla PH Community dedicates the second installment of the MozillaPH Social Media Series to current women and aspiring women bloggers from all the fields on interest. In this small meet-up we aim to get to know ladies who are into social media to share tips and tools that can help in their hobby/profession. We also aim to introduce Mozilla's efforts on women empowerment and other activities women can contribute to in the Mozilla PH community.
