DescriptionOSCAL is the only annual conference in Albania organized to promote software freedom, open source software, free culture and open knowledge. The conference has a multi-track approach with 2 parallel talks, 2 workshops tracks and community meetups. The 6th edition of the conference is taking on 18th & 19th of May 2019 and will gather FLOSS users, developers, academics, governmental agencies. Participation and attendance is free of charge and the organizing team is fully volunteer-based.
