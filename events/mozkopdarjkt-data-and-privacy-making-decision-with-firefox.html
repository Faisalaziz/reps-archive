TimezoneAsia/Jakarta
DescriptionTelemetry is non-personal data about the performance & usage of Firefox sent to Mozilla while you use Firefox browser. If you like data & want to know how Mozilla treats data to support various decisions, then this event is perfect for you! On this occasion, Georg Fritzsche (Engineering Manager, Firefox Telemetry) will share his experience on how Mozilla collects and uses data from Telemetry to make product-related decisions.
