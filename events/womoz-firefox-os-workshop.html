DescriptionThe Mozilla Philippines Community invites you to join us on Saturday, 23 November 2013 at the Coffee Bean and Tea Leaf Two E-com Tower (near Mall of Asia) in Pasay City for an all-girl mini workshop on Firefox OS, Mozilla's open source operating system for the mobile web. This workshop is NOT a competition; it's an opportunity to start creating mobile apps for Firefox Marketplace and the coming Firefox OS mobile devices. We'll introduce you to the tools you need to get started.
