DescriptionThe 2013 edition of the Smartmobil conference in Budapest will be held on April 4th. This year, they have developed a dedicated startup section, looking for the region’s best mobile startups and bringing a group of international investors and accelerators in the audience. Although the main focus of the conference seems to be on the Hungarian tech scene, the organizers specifically state they are accepting applications from “neighbouring countries”.
