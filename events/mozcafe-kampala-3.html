DescriptionThis is part of a series of our monthly MozCafe meetups. As a community, we are restructuring our community into functional areas so that we can find better ways to be more effective and create impact. The purpose of this MozCafe is to account for accountability, evaluate our selves and track progress and see if you are making progress with the various functional areas. These are some of the functional areas we have created; Firefox OS, Localization, WoMoz, MDN, and Webmaker.
