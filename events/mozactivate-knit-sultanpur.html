DescriptionThis is an introductory event at KNIT. This is the first Mozilla event in Sultanpur, Uttar Pradesh. After the basic intro to what WebVR is, we will move on to understand which libraries are their to help build the scenes in WebVR. Continuing on this, an introduction and hands on session will be delivered on how to use A-Frame JS to build a VR that can be loaded up on the web. We will explain webCompat, webExtensions, about campus clubs and other projects of Mozilla.
