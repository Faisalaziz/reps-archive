DescriptionThis will be our 2015 community meetup. We aim by this meetup to fix the agenda for the year and work on the remaining tasks for our main projects : We are observing many departures within the Mozilla Tunisia kernel for diverse reasons. We want via this meeting organize what's next for the community and have dedicated time slot to work on our recruitment strategy and finalize the launch of our website project. It's going to be a strategy meetup with diverse workshops
