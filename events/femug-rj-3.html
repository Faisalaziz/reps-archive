DescriptionFemug is a Brazil wide type of event that brings front-end developers together and has no defined format. It is quite like an unconference type of event. Femugs are small, many Brazilian capitals have monthly Femug meetings. Femug stands for Front-End Meetup User Group. Atteendees are encouraged to give lightning talks and everyone discuss the topic after the talks.
