DescriptionIftar is a religious culture for Muslim society. In Bangladesh it has a huge potentiality for different communities. For sharing ideas & discuss different issues Iftar is a great way to communicate. Last year we arranged the first MozIftar in Dhaka. We planned a year on that meetup. That planning was successful by many cases. This year we are planning for arranging three event in three cities (Dhaka, Chittagong & Khulna). All these cities has a huge number of potential Mozillians.
