DescriptionChicago City of Learning presents Destination Chicago featuring an all-day Maker Party. The space is full of stations and mini-sessions resulting with a “make”. Designed for youth from ages 4- 24, attendees, kids young and old, are able to pick and choose the stations that most draw them in. In this way, they make their way through the room, sampling, observing or diving into the offerings. For more on Chicago City of Learning & Destination Chicago: Check out explorechi.org FREE
