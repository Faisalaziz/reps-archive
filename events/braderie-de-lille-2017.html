DescriptionLike each year, the local Chtinux LUG in Lille (Northern France) invites us to its "free software village" in the middle of the Braderie de Lille, a gigantic flea market occupying the whole city center during the first weekend of September. More than 2.5 million people are expected to visit the braderie. A local volunteer presence for Mozilla was there in 2008, 2009, 2011,2012 2014 and 2015
