Start2014-10-09T14:00:00Z

TimezoneAmerica/Panama
DescriptionThis is the biggest event about Free Software in Central America. Each year change country. This year destination is Chitré, Panamá. Guillermo Movia will have a keynote session and Mozilla Community will have a space where we could create our agenda for two days. Also we are using this event to create the first Central America Mozilla's Communities meetup, inviting people from Costa Rica, Nicaragua, Honduras, El Salvador and Panamá.
