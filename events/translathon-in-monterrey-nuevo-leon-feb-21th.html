TimezoneAmerica/Monterrey
DescriptionWe proposed Translathon event, an activitie to make the localization of Firefox and creation of Wikipedia articles, in this case in Nahuatl, Hñahñú, Mixe, Mixteco, Mazahua and Tenek. The main objective is form community committed to support the localization, looking for awareness in our society about the importance of language preservation and the important issue of digital inclusion of indigenous communities. It calls on key dates such as February 21, the International Day Native Languages.
