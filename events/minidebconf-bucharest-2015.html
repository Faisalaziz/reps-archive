MiniDebConf Bucharest 2015

Organized byStefania Ioana Chiorean
Start2015-05-16T09:00:00Z
End2015-05-17T15:00:00Z
TimezoneEurope/Bucharest
DescriptionOn the weekend of May 16th and 17th, Bucharest will host a Mini DebConf with both talks and social events, to which everyone in Debian is invited but the speakers in the talks are all people who identify themselves as female. We consider this important to: Encourage women who haven't yet given their first DebConf talk Provide role models for women who are interested in contributing Debunk the myth that there are not enough women who can give talks in DebConf
