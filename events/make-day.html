DescriptionJan 26 is Indian Republic Day. The holiday is observed throughout India with flag-hoisting ceremonies, parades and cultural events. Indians celebrate the day by displaying the national flag on their attire and bonding with family and friends. The Mozilla India Community wants to celebrate by organizing Make Day especially for the day, and preparing some makes that you can remix to participate in the celebration. We’re calling it the “Make Day”, and we hope everyone takes part!
