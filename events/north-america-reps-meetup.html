DescriptionSince the inception of the Mozilla Reps program, there have numerous events that brought Reps together to foster community and collaboration. Among them were the MozCamps in Europe and Asia, local regional meetups, and country-level meetings. The North America Reps meetup aims to follow what the previous events have started and tailor a plan of action that is relevant to North American communities.
