DescriptionWoMoz AppSprint is to introduce girls interested in learning to code the free and open web language. The goal of this event is to introduce Firefox OS and Firefox Marketplace, and share knowledge about the tools available to help developers get started building apps to submit it to the Marketplace. Also, our other goal is to empower women from our community into Open Source and Mozilla.
