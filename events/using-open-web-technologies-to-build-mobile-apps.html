DescriptionThe workshop is dedicated to attendees at the InfoEducatie national contest (infoeducatie.ro) which take place on 28 July - 3 August 2013 on Galaciuc, Vrancea. Annual, this contest, gather many high school students (between 150 and 200 people in total) from all Romanian important schools. In our workshop we will talk about Mozilla's technologies, will introduce the Firefox OS and preview phones developed and talk about how to be involved and contribute to Mozilla.
