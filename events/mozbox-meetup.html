Mozbox Meetup

Organized byAke Nygren
Start2015-12-03T10:30:00Z
End2015-12-03T14:30:00Z
TimezoneEurope/Stockholm
DescriptionAn international makerbox meetup at Bibliotheekservice Fryslân. Participants will be Mozilla reps, Fab lab reps, library developers from the Netherlands and Sweden. Apart from an introduction to Frysklab (the world's first mobile library Fab Lab) the participants will be invited to some tinkering with a set of digital literacy focused makerboxes from myMakerbox.es. The project leader Åke Nygren will be on hand explaining how the boxes are linked to digital literacies and Mozilla tools.
