DescriptionThe term "Progressive Web App" refers to web applications built with a group of technologies, such as service workers, and push notifications, that can bring native-like performance and user experience to web apps. You get a chance to have a hands-on whole-day intro workshop into all these exciting new technologies and get started building your own installable, responsive, engaging progressive webapp with the help of experienced mentors from Mozilla.
