DescriptionThis event will bring together over 200 Nigeria youth service corp members (NYSC), graduates and students to translate Firefox to Hausa, learn more about Mozilla and have Firefox and Firefox add-on's installed for them. Through this event, we will strengthen the Mozilla contributor base in Nigeria. Attendees will be leave knowledgeable about Mozilla and even more importantly how to start contributing to Mozilla.
