DescriptionWe have been localizing various Mozilla products from several years. Now we are in the verge of releasing Fx19 Bengali build in February. bn-BD is added to MDN, which also need to be translated. We will teach how to localize Mozilla products and then we will do a sprint for "MDN Documentation", "Mozilla.Lang" and "Firefox OS". 21st February is the International Mother Language Day, declared by UNESCO. We arranging the l10n sprint with the "Save our language on the Web" spirit.
