DescriptionThe event is spearheaded by e27.co, a Singapore-based media organization focused on the Asian technology startup industry. The Manila Satellite is a conference and pitching competition that will prelude to e27's big event in June 4-5 -- Echelon 2013 -- where the "regional" champs get to deck it out in Singapore as part of a 2-3 day conference. The Manila leg will be held on Apr 13 (Sat). Expected participants include local and foreign Startup Founders, Investors, Media and Tech luminaries.
