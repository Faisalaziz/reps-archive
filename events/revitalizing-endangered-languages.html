DescriptionThe conference seeks to explore new approaches to language revitalization, such as the development of research models based on close collaboration between scholars and the native speakers of endangered languages as well as their communities. The plan is to bring together and foster vital networking among field researchers in language revitalization and documentation with members of speech communities.
