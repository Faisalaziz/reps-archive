TimezoneAsia/Taipei
DescriptionNCC (Taiwan's FCC) had draft 2 new digital convergence acts, which will be the the main law of internet governance. There are regulations about net neutrality, transparency of traffic adjustment and public multistakeholder approach, as well as national security and DMCA-like takedown. Mozilla Taiwan Community had co-work with others to host this event, we will lead the discussion line by line through the whole draft acts, and hope to create an opinion afterward.
