DescriptionFirefox OS launching campaign through the Orange KLIFF mobile continues for week-6 in Mauritius. This is the 6th and last week of a public launch campaign, organised by Orange Mauritius from 9 May 2015, which will last for 6 weeks till July 25, 2015. These events have been conducted in different regions (cities and villages) of the Mauritius. Mozilla Mauritius team will be at the Orange Lor Baz event which will be held on 25 July 2015 in the South of Mauritius at Mahebourg.
