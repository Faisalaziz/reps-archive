DescriptionMozilla QA Bugday Dhaka is a regional event to encourage contributors to contribute in QA. It will be an offsite event to help people to contribute in QA. Participant will be guided from the basic of Bugzilla to Testing bugs, Verifying bugs and some other aspects of QA . It is a 2 Days event and participants will be mentored all the way so that they can be comfortable with all the basic procedure of QA and can jump in to make Firefox better.
