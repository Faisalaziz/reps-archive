Test pilot study: Firefox for Android Community Campaign Challenge

Organized byOskar Ivanic
Start2012-12-01T11:00:00Z
End2012-12-20T11:00:00Z
TimezoneEurope/Belgrade
DescriptionMozilla Serbia is going to do a test pilot study with poster that were created by Aleksandra and Oskar. Poster will be put on various places such as bus stops, universities and other places that are visited by a lot of people or where a lot of people pass by. Poster will make a clear call for people to download Firefox for Android. Subotica city will be our start where we will track QR codes to see how many people are downloading Firefox, and from which places is being most downloaded.
