DescriptionDuring the conference LinuxDays 2016, we decided to take one hour to talk with other open-source software l10n communities about restarting L10N.cz. L10N.cz used to be a place for sharing Czech l10n documents, thoughts, tips and best practices. We would like to raise it again to improve our communication between communities and cooperate close in areas like promotion, social media, knowledge building etc.
