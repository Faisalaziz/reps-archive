TimezoneAfrica/Kampala
DescriptionThe Mozilla Festival East Africa (MEA15) is a 3 day maker party that brings together educators, innovators, learners and makers from East Africa and beyond to share experiences and build the web together laying a foundation for growth of the internet and information of technology in Africa. This is an annual event that is intended to spread across Africa helping address the technological challenges faced in the African continent through meaningful collaborations across academic disciplines.
