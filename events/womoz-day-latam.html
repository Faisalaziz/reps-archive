DescriptionThe main theme of this event is "The inclusion of th women in the technology and the participation of women in the Mozilla community". This is an event promoted by enthusiasts of Mozilla women and free software in Nicaragua and Latin America to increase women's participation. The event will take place at the National Engineering University. In this congress the participants can assist to workshops, talks and other activities imparted by technology enthusiasts and other proffesionals.
