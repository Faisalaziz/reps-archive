DescriptionThe event will consist of talks, focused on introducing the world of FOSS and Mozilla to the students. The students will gain insights into Mozilla's mission to keep the power of the Web in people hands and also the numerous projects which Mozilla is working on. The students will be introduced to Mozilla's new mobile operating system - Firefox OS, ahead of its forthcoming launch in India. The students will also be guided on the different ways of contributing to the Mozilla projects like Firefox.
