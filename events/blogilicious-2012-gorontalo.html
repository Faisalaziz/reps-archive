DescriptionBlogilicious, is a seminar and workshop event in 7 cities in Indonesia with targeting of 200 bloggers per-city that aims to encourage young generation of Indonesia to be a "positive blogger" that that share positive contents/infos/news to Internet citizen. Blogilicious welcome Mozilla Indonesia as one if the community who support open source, user privacy, and internet safety. That's why Mozilla Indonesia were invited to Blogilicious in those 7 cities.
