DescriptionUser Experience (UX) of websites and apps is now more important than ever -- customers expectations are getting higher with every well-designed app and as developers, designers, and innovators, delivering top-notch UD design is a key ingredient to success. This workshop aims to introduce the contemporary UX design processes used by companies from Silicon Valley to Bangalore. Participants will spend the time on covering the theory and application of UX design concepts.
