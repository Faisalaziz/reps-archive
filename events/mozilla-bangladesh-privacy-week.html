DescriptionTo celebrate Privacy Day, Inspired by Mozilla India, Mozilla Bangladesh is organizing Week long activities on Privacy. We are initiating our "Privacy Week" at the end of today's very "Data Privacy Day"; our week starts 28th Jan and shall end on 4th Feb 2016. In this Privacy Week we will be reaching everyone around us to share our thoughts about Privacy and help everyone to learn about privacy in our daily life as well as in our Digital Life.
