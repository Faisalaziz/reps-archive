Organized byPriyanka Nag

TimezoneAsia/Kolkata
DescriptionCultra is an annual cultural and tech fest of CMR Institute of Technology. This time, they have invited Mozilla to be a part of this fest. We have decided to host a Webmaker hackathon for the same. The facilitaros will demonstrate a Webmaker tool for the initial 10mins and then open a contest for the next 45mins, where the students will have to create something using the same tool. Each round will have elimination and at the end, the top one or two groups will be awarded some prize.
