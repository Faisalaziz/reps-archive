Mozilla and our experiments around Smart Home

Organized byMichael Kohler
Start2016-03-31T16:00:00Z

TimezoneEurope/Zurich
DescriptionLast december it became clear that Mozilla will shift its focus to Connected Devices. In the first two months of 2016 it became clear as well, that Mozilla will sunset Firefox OS after the version 2.6 release in May. Even though the smartphone might still be relevant in the IoT, we will focus on how we can use the power of the Web for IoT. In this talk Michael will show a few demos (might still be alpha by then) and show you the new, exiting path for the future.
