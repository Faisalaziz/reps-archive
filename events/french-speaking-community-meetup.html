DescriptionThe French-speaking community is growing and now concerns over 15 countries. Over 200 people are working together easily because they share the same language, and they are contributing more and more. But sometimes, it's hard because of the distance, and because of the jet lag. So this event would be a fantastic way to share our knowledge, and to prepare the future events in these 15 countries, IRL. We would also work together on MDN, Webmaker, and of course, Firefox OS!
