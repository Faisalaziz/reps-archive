DescriptionHillhacks is a two week long event in Dharamsala, India with participants from all around India and all around the world. It's organized by CCC and freaklabs as well as participants from MIT Media Lab, Tibetan Children's Village, Dharamsala International Film Festival, Ghoomakad hackerspace, and many more. The Mozilla India community sees this as a great opportunity to reachout to the Tibetian as well as local kids from the Dharamsala area and teach them some quick tricks of webmaking.
