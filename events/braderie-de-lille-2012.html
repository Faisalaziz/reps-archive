DescriptionEach year, the local LUG in Lille (Northern France) sets up a small "free software village" inside the Braderie de Lille, a gigantic flea market occupying the whole city center during the first weekend of september. More than two million people are expected to visit the braderie. Other attendees include Ubuntu-fr, FSF Europe, Wikimedia France, and more. A local volunteer presence for Mozilla was there in 2008, 2009 and 2011.
