DescriptionThe first WoMoz event in BR will happen in a small city on the countryside called Gaurama. It has only 5000 inhabitants and discovered the importance that learning the web can have on their inhabitants' lives, and it's putting a lot of effort on that, as the revitalization of the city's telecentro on the countryside and the digital baptism of almost 10% of its population, focusing on females. This is undoubtedly an event to be remembered in the city surroundings and maybe even in broader circles
