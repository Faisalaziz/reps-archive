DescriptionVIII Latinamerican festival of open source installation. In Bogotá, will be 4 simultaneus Flisol: a Big one and 3 small. We will participate in the big one. Our idea is to be part of the sponsors group, contributing with t-shirts for Installers (80) and Logistic people (32, for the big and smalls).
