DescriptionThe first OpenXLab meetup at Mozilla Berlin about Open Hardware, the Internet of Things and Open Tech startups. We have exciting speakers including Lorenzo Pirritano, Prashanth Makaram (from Fabulyzer) and Larisa Kryuchkova (UVIZR). You are invited to a reception with beverages and snacks after the presentations. The idea behind OpenXLab is to build future labs where people from various backgrounds can experiment, create new things and develop open hardware for the Internet of things.
