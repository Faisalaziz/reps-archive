DescriptionSoftware Freedom Day (SFD) is an annual worldwide celebration of the free/open-source software. SFD is a public education effort, not only to celebrate the alleged virtues of free and open source software, but also to encourage its use, to the benefit of the public. In Nepal, Software Freedom Day is being celebrated since last seven years. Every year we celebrate the day with several events like documentary show, distro demo, and many more..
