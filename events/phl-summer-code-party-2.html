DescriptionA follow up event to the successful kickoff last June 23, and commemoration of one of Philippines' national heroes. This targets college students and professionals mostly living in the city and nearby towns. It aims to: - promote and encourage more Webmaker events in the local community - teach website hacking through the use of tools like Hackasaurus, Thimble and X-Ray Goggles - recruit more students to Student Reps program - expand the local User and Contributor Engagement Teams
