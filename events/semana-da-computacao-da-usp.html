DescriptionA week about technology and computers at the biggest university in Brazil. Many companies will be there to showcase their new products, to recruit new employees and to talk about their news. However, Mozilla will be there with a different purpose. We aim to bring Web Literacy through Webmaker for those who did not had the opportunity to learn yet. We aim to demonstrate how easy it is to become a Firefox OS developer. We'll get our crowd craving for more.
