DescriptionIn celebration of the Maker Party Season and back-to-school season, the Mozilla Philippines Community organize a Maker Party for students around/near the metro, where we'll introduce the Webmaker project and its objective which is to teach the web and and promote the web literacy. With this event, students will learn how to use the webmaker tools, that they can use with their other own projects and in learning how to make/teach the web.
