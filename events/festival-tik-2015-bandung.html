DescriptionFestival TIK 2015 (2015 ICT Festival) is a nationwide festival arranged by Ministry of Communication & Informatics in collaboration with "Relawan TIK" or ICT Volunteers, Bandung City Government and local IT community. This is the fourth Festival that was held ever and Bandung will host the event. Mozilla Indonesia community will have a presence in this event by opening #MozBooth & having a workshop class about Webmaker for free.
