DescriptionHindi Urdu L10n Sprint is an initiative to onboard new contributors who can contribute to SuMo(Hindi/Urdu) and Pontoon. Gathering of l10n Communities (Hindi and Urdu) to plan new programs and initiatives, work on priority projects, educate and aware them of new tools available for localizing projects. The main aim of this event is to train the participants to be able to do quality contributions to Mozilla L10n projects.
