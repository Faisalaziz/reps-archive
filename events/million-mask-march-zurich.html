DescriptionCurrently there is a discussion in Swiss parliament to reform the current law regarding digital law enforcement. In privacy-concerned circles this reform is seen as a massive decrement of privacy of Swiss people. One of the means to increase the enforcement would be a trojan horse-like tool which would be installed on people's computers. Therefore there will be a demonstration in Zurich to get us heard and get as many attention to the topic as possible, so not only geeks see the problem.
