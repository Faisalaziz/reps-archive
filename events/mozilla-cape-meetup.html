DescriptionThis is a community meet-up for Personal Leadership in Mozilla open to everyone. The purpose is to establish a common language and set of practices for participation development, different personalities/leadership traits. We want to build on these during and beyond the meet-up. The hope is that it triggers healthy discussions on what leadership means in Mozilla.
