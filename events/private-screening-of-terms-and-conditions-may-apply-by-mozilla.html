Organized byJeff Beatty
Start2014-07-17T00:00:00Z
End2014-07-17T02:30:00Z
TimezoneAmerica/Denver
DescriptionMozilla invites faculty and administrators from Utah institutions to a private screening of the web privacy documentary, "Terms and Conditions May Apply." This event is for Mozilla to present a privacy-oriented pilot program of donating public screening licenses of the documentary to Utah institutions.The goal of this is to increase public web literacy and public dialogue concerning online privacy issues throughout Utah. We will follow the screening with a Q&A about the educational program.
