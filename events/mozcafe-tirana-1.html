DescriptionMozCafé Tirana #1 is the first event of its kind in Albania, where the local Mozilla community gathers to talk about everything Mozilla related, in an informal way, without forgetting to have fun of course! In the first event we will have a look at the brand new FoxConn tablet, which Mozilla Rep Elio Qoshi received as part of the Tablet Contribution Program. We will also discuss about the Firefox OS Launch Team which has been created.
