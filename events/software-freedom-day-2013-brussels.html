DescriptionSoftware Freedom Day is a worldwide celebration of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS). Our goal in this celebration is to educate the worldwide public about the benefits of using high quality FOSS in education, in government, at home, and in businesses - in short: everywhere! We will have a session on Firefox OS, presenting Mozilla's mission, the Firefox OS project, apps development and demonstrating phones.
