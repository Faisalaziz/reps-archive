DescriptionWoMoz known as MozGirls in Mozilla Bangladesh, will have an important event to engage more girls in tech and in the contribution team of Mozilla. This event is basically for the core and newbie MozGirls who would be trained and and then forwarded to different pathways to contribute. We will also have planning of next 6 months during this event on how we can organize events in different campuses of Bangladesh focusing different contribution pathways.
