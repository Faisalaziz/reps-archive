DescriptionThe Debian Day, which is celebrated every August 16, commemorates the birth anniversary of the Debian Project (in English, Debian Project), which is responsible for the creation and maintenance of Debian GNU / Linux distribution organization focused on GNU / Linux and GNU utilities. Founded in 1993 by Ian Murdock, the project has grown from a small group of hackers to what is now a worldwide community dedicated to the maintenance and development of this operating system group.
