Connecting 100 women first time user to a Healthy Web in Kolkata

Organized byBiraj Karmakar
Start2017-06-01T04:30:00Z
End2017-08-31T13:30:00Z
TimezoneAsia/Kolkata
DescriptionIn this workshop, we will invite 100 women who are from low-income family background, have not started using smart phone. We will do a research a how they can join in Healthy Web. Then we will teach them through a resource that will improve their lives and societies. We need to find what are the barriers divide them to adopt online lives. We will give further skills to overcome that issue. Other than that we will give them digital skills and privacy issue.
