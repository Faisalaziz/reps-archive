DescriptionComilla is a district of Bangladesh where Mozilla didn't arrive yet. So, that's why to build a new community we are starting with Contributor Mentoring Day at Comilla first. It will be held on "Comilla Lalmai College". Main goals are: 1. To create a new Community. 2. To create a new FSA club in Comilla. 3. Make them able to organize their own event. 4. Create a good volunteer team so that we can organize a big event of thousand participants in future.
