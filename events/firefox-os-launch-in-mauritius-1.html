DescriptionFirefox OS was scheduled for launching in Mauritius in May 2015 through the Orange KLIFF mobile. The launching event is being organised by Orange Mauritius from 9 May 2015, which will last for 6 weeks till July 25, 2015. These events will be run in different regions (cities and villages) of the Mauritius. Mozilla Mauritius will join this event to promote Firefox OS. The next launch event will be held in the East of Mauritius in Flacq.
