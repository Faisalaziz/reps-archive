TimezoneEurope/Stockholm
DescriptionA pop up exhibition, courtesy of Tactical Tech Collective with support from Mozilla - about online privacy in an age of big data, IoT and AI. We run the exhibition in the Hornstull library in conjunction with an authours talk on the recently published book "Internet is Broken - Silicon Valley and the Crisis of Democracy" by Martin Gelin and Karin Petterssion. The book is nominated to one of Sweden's most prestigious literary awards "Augustpriset" so we are expecting a lot of attention.
