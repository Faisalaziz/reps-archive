DescriptionImagiLAN events were standard LAN parties and gaming contests, but they have become events promoting everything open, such as net neutrality, free software, open hardware, open standards etc. Many FLOSS related local non-profits will attend, have a booth and give talks to present their work to the public. There will be talks and workshops about open hardware, arduino, the open web, FLOSS and net neutrality.
