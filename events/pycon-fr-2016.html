DescriptionSince 2007, thanks to the AFPy ("Association Francophone Python"), French-speaking users of the Python language are gathering together during a few days to talk about their experiences in the field, learn from each other and show off new tricks. PyCon-fr ( http://www.pycon.fr ) became a reference event to learn Python, refine one's skills, meet maintainers of popular libraries… and essentially meet and have a pleasant time together. Mozilla is a platinum sponsor of the event
