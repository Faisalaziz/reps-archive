DescriptionMoz Hack Night is an initiative by Mozilla Phoenix Club RGI. It is an opportunity for students to contribute to Mozilla Projects. It is a 12 hours hackathon which focuses on involving more student developers in the Open Source World. Projects range in platform and application, including elements of web development, mobile applications, and many others. Students work for around 12 hours on a programming project under the guidance of various Mentors on 29th September 2018.
