DescriptionCraft booth at the Mozilla Summit to make a necklace, bracelet, or charm with the number of your first bug in bugzilla.mozilla.org. I will invite people to look up their "first bug" reported, commented on, triaged, or patched. Then make a nifty memento to wear. We will also make a welcoming space for Mozilla fiber arts lovers, and invite them to join the Ravelry Mozilla craft group.
