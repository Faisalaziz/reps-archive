DescriptionIn celebration of the women's month, the Mozilla Philippines Community invites all women to join us on Saturday, 15 March 2014 at Commune in Makati for an all-girl mini workshop on Firefox OS, Mozilla's open source operating system for the mobile web. This workshop is NOT a competition; it's an opportunity to start creating mobile apps for Firefox Marketplace and the coming Firefox OS mobile devices. We'll introduce you to the tools you need to get started.
