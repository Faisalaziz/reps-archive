DescriptionSoftware Freedom Day (SFD) is an annual worldwide celebration of Free Software. SFD is a public education effort with the aim of increasing awareness of Free Software and its virtues and encouraging its use. Last year in Sri Lanka we have hosted 2 SFD at National School of Business Management ( NSBM ) and Rajarata University of Sri Lanka ( RUSL ) , and this time all Mozilla campus clubs around Sri Lanka teamed up to host one SFD with everyone.
