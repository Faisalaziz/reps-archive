DescriptionMozilla is going to support the foundation of an IT-based club in a school with over a 1000 students. The main aim of the club is to promote the idea of the open web for everyone. The students will get an opportunity to attend weekly lectures on computing and technology, provided by professional mentors from Mozilla. At the club pupils will learn a wide range of topics from web languages and apps to the basics of IT security.
