DescriptionWikuFest is an annual education festival talks about ICT. Mozilla Indonesia were participated in their previous events in 3 consecutive year. This year WikuFest's theme is "New Beginnings". WikuFest is a place for sharing knowledge, networking, wisdom, and other technologies. WikuFest is organized by the school's alumni for the students in the SMK Telkom Sandhy Putra Malang, to encourage the students to learn more about IT and open the world wide :)
