DescriptionMozilla Mauritius is organising this training/presentation event for children aged between 11-15. It is going to be an introduction to webmaking which will be held for the first in the Eastern region of Mauritius in Flacq. This session was schedule since the beginning of this year following an invitation from the school teacher. This event will be mainly a showcase presentation about webmaking and help the children to do something on the web.
