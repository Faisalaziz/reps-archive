DescriptionIn celebration of the Maker Party Season, the Mozilla Philippines Community organize a Maker Party for teachers/professors, where the attendees can have a deepen understanding what is Webmaker project of Mozilla. At the end of the session we'll expect to increase the number of Wemaker mentors.
