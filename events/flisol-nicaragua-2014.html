DescriptionThe Latin American Festival of Free Software Installation (FLISoL) event is the dissemination of Free Software in Latin America's largest. In Nicaragua the event will be in the Centroamericana University at April 26th, 2014. From: 8:00 am to 04:00 pm. Mozilla Nicaragua will have an stand, a conference about firefox os and webbaps and a firefox os application development from linux workshop.
