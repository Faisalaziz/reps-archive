DescriptionFirefoxOS app hackathon in Uganda is to introduce firefoxOS to developers and students who can create or who want to create apps for firefoxOS. We shall take them through the web technologies to be used(css,HTML and Javascript) , how to package the application, using the simulator, installing them on the keon devices and posting on the marketplace. It will be learning, hacking and celebrating the open web
