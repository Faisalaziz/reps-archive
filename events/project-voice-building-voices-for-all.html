DescriptionWe are celebrating the World Wide Web 30th Anniversary, but the most of the content on the Internet is in English 50-60% and the native speakers are 5-6% of the world population. With voice assistants this becomes a problem for non-native speakers which forces them to rely on a foreign language to learn. And the existing technology doesn't support languages which are diverse and also underserved in many communities which cripple them for using the technology.
