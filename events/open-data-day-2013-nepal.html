DescriptionOpen communities of Nepal are planning on celebrating Open Data Day jointly this year. 1. FOSS Nepal 2. Mozilla Nepal 3. Wikipedia/Wikimedia Nepal 4. Open Knowledge Foundation Nepal 5. Open Learning Exchange Nepal (NGO, Non-profit) Mozilla Nepal will be collaborating with OKFN. OKFN will be doing data visualization using Webmaker and their API. FSAs will be doing work on webmaker, it will help FSAs who have already or haven't used webmaker to learn practically.
