DescriptionWe are organizing this event for some of our new contributors who are unaware of maker party. Getting a venue, gathering everyone together has often been a problem both from the logistical view point as well as for the participants. We thus thought of experimenting with an online version of Maker Party, where anyone with a decent internet connection, from any part of the world can join in and make the web.
