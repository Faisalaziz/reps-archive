Organized byMonica Bonilla
Start2016-06-30T15:00:00Z
End2016-07-04T17:00:00Z
TimezoneAmerica/Lima
DescriptionIn this event we will call speakers of different variants of the Quechua Cusco language to translate strings and validate progress. In turn it is to work on localization in Quechua chanka language reaching a variable representing 4 different version. Training, advice and support in the use of Pootle, plus guidelines for creating new terms and use of metaphors will be given.
