DescriptionT3LAB organizes, in collaboration with the Mozilla Italy Association, the first event in Emilia-Romagna entirely dedicated to Firefox OS, the new mobile operating system for smartphones, completely open source based on web standards. The event will be divided into two sections: the morning will be dedicated to technical talks by Mozilla representatives, while in the afternoon the staff of Mozilla will support participants in developing their own application for Firefox OS.
