Mozilla Bangladesh tour in Comilla

Organized byBelayet Hossain
Start2017-03-10T04:00:00Z
End2017-03-10T15:00:00Z
TimezoneAsia/Dhaka
DescriptionComilla is the second-largest city of eastern Bangladesh and 100 km away from capital city Dhaka and is one of the three oldest cities in Bangladesh. The objective of this event is to spread Mozilla mission, promote Mozilla products and create awareness about Open Web in Comilla. Also, this event will enable contributors for Mozilla l10n in bn-BD and recruit new contributors in l10n from Comilla.The tour will initiate and strengthen Mozilla clubs in different institutions at Comilla region.
