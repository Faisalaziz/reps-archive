DescriptionThis curriculum was created for a Defense and Detoxification event for women in Mexico City to teach digital women how to protect themselves online. The curriculum can be tailored for women anywhere. This is not the Internet I want for women. I do not want us to struggle to maintain our online presence or fear what could happen to us if we stayed in dangerous spaces. I want an ecosystem that includes our differences. We are far from achieving this.
