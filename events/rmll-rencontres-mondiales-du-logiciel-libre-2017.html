DescriptionRMLL or LSM in English (for Libre Software Meeting) is more than 200 conferences along 5 days made by worldwide speakers. It has been more than 10 years since the LSM started to put themselves at the disposal of Libre communities, professionals and general public ! Sharing of knowledge, freedom of information, community spirit, discussions, technological progress : every year the Libre Software Meeting (LSM) follows the Libre philosophy.
