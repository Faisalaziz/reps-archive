DescriptionMarch is declared Women's Month and we decided through an event to celebrate women in the world of technology. This event is International Women's Day meetup in Yamoussoukro, aims to introduce the technologies, web universe and the code to the women (Students or not). This is real opportunity to grow the number of women in the tech area in our city and to invite female tech lead to bring our experiences to other women.
