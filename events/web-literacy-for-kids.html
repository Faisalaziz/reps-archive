DescriptionA web Literacy program is planned for the students of a night school named Nanavare School, Wadala, Mumbai. On every Republic Day (January 26), a NGO conduct event at the school where all the activities are done by students. The volunteers take part in teaching students some new skills that help them go a step forward in their career. This time we have planned for web literacy. Students who are already aware of social networking must know how to use the internet properly.
