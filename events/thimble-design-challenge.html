DescriptionCultura is an annual cultural and tech fest of CMR Institute of Technology with a footfall of over 2000 people. This time, they have invited Mozilla to be a part of this fest to conduct a design challenge. The facilitators will demonstrate the use of Thimble and then open up a contest for the next 90 minutes, where the students will have to create/remix a web hack. Each round will have elimination and at the end, the top one or two groups will be awarded prizes and goodies.
