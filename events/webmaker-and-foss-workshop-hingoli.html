DescriptionThis workshop aims at education young college going kids about FOSS, Mozilla and open web. Its is an effort to build a Mozilla and FOSS community in and around Hingoli. The event would also include sessions on Mozilla Webmaker, hands-on with awesome webmaker tools like Thimble, X-Ray Goggles and Appmaker. Also educating them about FOSS , Mozilla Initiatives and Contributions, a bit about L10N and a small Q/A session.
