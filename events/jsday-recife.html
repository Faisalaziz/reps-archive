DescriptionSDay Recife was created to leverage the Javascript knowledge in the local region. The event's mission is to spread the word about the programming language mostly used around the world. There will be 8 hours of event in a single day, with more than 40 hours in total divided into 3 tracks of talks from local and non-local specialists, including some invited and some selected through the Call for Papers. Additionally, there will be 4 workshops in parallel.
