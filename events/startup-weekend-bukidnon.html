DescriptionStartup Weekend is a weekend-long, hands-on experience where startups and aspiring entrepreneurs gather in one place, pitch their ideas and find out if they are business viable. The three day event consists of pitching the idea, mentoring sessions with key people in the startup industry and presentation of ideas to investors and industry leaders. It allows anyone with an idea for a business to find potential co-founders and partners who can help build the business with little investment.
