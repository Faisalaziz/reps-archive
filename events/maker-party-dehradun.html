DescriptionMaker Party Dehradun is the second event from Mozilla Dehrdaun. We organised Maker Party UPES on February 20, 2015 in University of Petroleum & Energy Studies, Dehradun. This time, we have planned a city wide technical expo again in University of Petroleum & Energy Studies, Dehradun. We have inputs from 4 Firefox clubs in the city namely DIT Firefox Club, GEHU Firefox Club, DBIT Firefox Club and UPES Firefox Club. Maker Party Dehradun is in collaboration with UPES Open Source Community.
