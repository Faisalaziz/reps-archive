DescriptionCampus Party is considered by many the largest technology event in the world. It has hundreds of activities going on during its five days schedule. There are two areas on the event, the expo area with stands from many companies and the arena where the "campuseros" stay with the five stages, workshop areas and tables. Only campuseros can enter and stay at the arena. The arena has 24/7 schedule and the campuseros stay at tents there. This year there will be 8.000 campuseros there.
