DescriptionThe Mozilla Central America community and Mozilla hispano community will participate in the 11th Central American Free Software Conference (ECSL) 2019 and they will also organize the Mozilla Activate Day, both events in Guatemala. The volunteers of Mozilla have coordinated to participate in both events contemplating multiple activities in search of community growth in the region. The activities of the two events will be: talks, workshops and stands, which will be presented by Mozillians.
