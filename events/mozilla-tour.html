DescriptionMozilla Tour II is going to be the second tour after the one we had last February. Mozilla Mauritius will continue its mission to reach the youth and kids. The event will comprise of an introduction of Mozilla and its products to students and a Webmaker activity. We have been invited by the Global Rainbow Trust Foundation for an introduction to Mozilla and also to look into how to collaborate in the future. This will be definitely an opportunity for Mozilla Mauritius for partnership.
