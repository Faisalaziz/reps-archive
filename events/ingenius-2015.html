DescriptioninGenius is one of Bangalore's prominent college-level hackathon. After three successful iterations, inGenius 2015 is back on the 2nd and 3rd of October, 2015 to host 250+ ingenious hackers! The aim is to foster and bring up the developer community in colleges as well as provide them a platform to showcase their maker skills. It's an event where people from all India take part to create and build products of value. It's a flagship event being organized by PESIT South ACM chapter.
