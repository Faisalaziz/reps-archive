DescriptionLäromedelmässan (Education material fair) is one of the largest education events in Sweden taking place in Eriksbergshallen Gothenburg with about 2000 professional teachers attending the event. Läromedelmässan represent a forum to introduce Webmaker and Mozilla to an audience who may have limited knowledge about Mozilla and can thus be hard for us to reach.
