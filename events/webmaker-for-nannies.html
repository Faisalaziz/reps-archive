Webmaker for Nannies

Organized byRegnard Raquedan
Start2013-04-21T13:00:00Z
End2013-04-28T20:00:00Z
TimezoneAmerica/Toronto
DescriptionThe concept is pretty simple: Enable nannies to become Webmaker coaches to their wards. The learning setting for them is very informal and the expectation ranges from sharing some bits of info about Webmaker initiatives to doing demos of the tools. The over-arching goal is to get their wards interested and motivated about Webmaking. The main benefit of this is a win-win situation for both the nanny and the ward: Both of them gain knowledge about Webmaking.
