DescriptionFOSScamp Balkans 2016 is an adventure with free open source geeks from communities all around the Balkans aside the beach. Three days of co-working for the community in the Balkans in what is expected to be an un-conference where different communities in the region will collaborate, present and host workshops about different free software projects including, but not limited to Mozilla Firefox and tech speakers program. FOSScamp requires active participation from al participants.
