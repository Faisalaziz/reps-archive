DescriptionUbuConLa is the Latin American edition of UbuCon. UbuConLA, as it was called from the beginning, is held annually in a Latin American city where the Open Source and Open Source community gathers to attend talks, workshops, networking and conferences around these issues, in order to create spaces for students, professionals and people who are interested in knowing everything related to new free technologies at hand of the different professionals working in the area.
