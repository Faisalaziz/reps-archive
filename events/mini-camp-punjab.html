DescriptionWe are organizing this event for all passionate people in and around Punjab, to tell them about the community as well as to motivate them for individual contributions to the Mozilla's projects and to the community. The main focus of this event is to build & grow the Mozilla Community in Punjab and encourage more and more newbie volunteers to join the mission, ensure productivity and quality in the contributions and plan for the future events & goals for the Mozilla Community Punjab.
