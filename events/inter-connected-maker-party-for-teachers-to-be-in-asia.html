DescriptionThis workshop is designed for teachers-to-be who want to introduce ICT to children in a unique way. Participants will learn various ways of having fun with children through “web-making” activities, acquire a wider view on ICT, and create their own lesson plan as a “Class-Recipe”. Anyone who is involved in teaching ICT related topics to people around them are welcome to the party!!
