DescriptionRaj Nilayam, Mozilla contributor from Bhopal, India has taken initiative to spread the awesomeness of Mozilla to his hometown and home state. Bihar is a state in India with low education rate and least web literacy. As a supporter to Mozilla's mission of creating web literacy Raj has taken initiative in Banka district of Bihar. Normal contents like; working of web, creating web pages, ip, www, etc would be taught in workshop. venue : Adwaita mission High school and P.L.K.S.S.Vidya Mandir.
