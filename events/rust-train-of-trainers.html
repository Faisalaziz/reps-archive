DescriptionThe Rust - Train of Trainers is a series of talks and workshops about Rust that aims more developers know the language and get familiar with the way of developing projects with it. After the event, attendees are commited to train others by organizing events following the format of the training or their own ideas. This event pretends to increase the participation in the Rust community and adoption of the language.
