DescriptionNgabuburit* with Mozilla: L10N Workshop Bandung. In this event, participants will learn how to contribute to Mozilla by localizing its projects to Bahasa Indonesia, Basa Sunda, or other languages in Indonesia. In the end of the event, organizers and participants will have an iftar together (break our ramadhan fast for the day). *)ngabuburit: an Indonesian slang word for time-killing activity meant to wait for iftar time.
