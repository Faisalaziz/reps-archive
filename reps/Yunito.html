Xavier Martorell

Bio# Biography My name is Xavier Martorell, I’m 21 years old and I'm from Mallorca, Spain. I’ve been collaborating in Mozilla since I joined Mozilla Hispano in 2013 when a window pop-up on my screen asking for collaboration in Mozilla which I was not willing to refuse. I started helping in the QA Area of Mozilla Hispano with Gabriela Montagu (gaby2300) who later on would become my mentor and a really good friend. Nowadays, I'm pushing the Mozilla Hispano community forward, introducing them to the new projects which Mozilla is working on as well as trying to engage as many members as possible. This wouldn't be possible without the help of my Mozilla Hispano fellows.
