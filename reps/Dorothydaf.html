Dorothee Danedjo Fouba

BioDorothee Danedjo Fouba is a Multimedia Journalist, Media Educator Engineer and Blogger on IT issues. Dorothee has won international awards for her work in ICT (Best African ICT Blog 2012&2013-African FOSS Reporter Award in 2012 and in 2010 as a Finalist- Techwoman Emerging Leader 2014, African Story Challenge 2014 Finalist, Business and Technology cycle) Dorothee holds a Master Degree in Computer Science and Communication and a Bachelor Degree in Journalism. As a Mozillian, she is leading Mozilla Cameroon community. Dorothee launched Firefox Student Ambassadors, WoMoz and Webmaker programs in Cameroon
