Unfortunately this serves as an example for one of the Reps program's biggest weaknesses: Level of activity among Mozilla Reps. Mozilla is a huge organization requiring a broad spectrum of activities from its contributors. While the Reps program is certainly not perfectly suited for everybody, I am sure there is a spot for anybody to contribute to Mozilla's goals and mission. This email is a heads-up for the "Activity Campaign" which will be kicked-off soon. The campaign aims at separating active Mozilla Reps from inactive ones, potentially re-activating dormant Reps. Those who are found to be inactive by the time the campaign ends will be moved to the Reps Alumni group (https://reps.mozilla.org/people/alumni/). Personally, I welcome this initiative. It serves as a way to figure out who is still willing and able to contribute towards the program's goals. Please watch out for the official announcements of the Activity Campaign and do not hesitate to contact me in case you have any questions. Best regards, Hugo - Mozilla Reps Mentor ---
