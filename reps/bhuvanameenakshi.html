Bio

Brief Bio

WebXR evangelist

Joined Mozilla India Community in 2014 as a Firefox Student Ambassador and started to actively participate in my regional community, MozillaTN. In Mozilla Tamil Nadu Community I contributed for QA days, design team, localisation, SUMO and then found my passion towards WebVR and started doing constant contributions towards this stream. Was also part of the Add-ons Featured Advisory board.


I became a Mozillian Rep during the year 2018 and I am evangelising on WebVR/ Mozilla Mixed Reality at renowned institutions and do a lot of speaker sessions and workshops on the same. Also I guide colleges for Campus Clubs activities .

Help Firefox understand what’s in the dark funnel, by participating in this campaign and spreading the light!
