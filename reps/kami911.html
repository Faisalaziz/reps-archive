Kalman Szalai

BioKAMI has been contributing to Mozilla community since 2004. He primary works on third party extension and SeaMonkey localization into Hungarian language. He also organizes release parties and gives a lecture about Mozilla and its products on other events, release parties and festivals. He also writes articles to Hungarian Mozilla Community site. KAMI is very committed about Free Softwares so he contributes such projects like LibreOffice, Ubuntu, kdenlive, Compiz, system-config-printer and so on. He works as a system administrator in a development company which provides cutting edge technology products based on telecommunication and he uses these key technologies in his daily life.
