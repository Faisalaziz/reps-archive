Jyotsna Gupta

Bio**Jyotsna**, an Open Source Enthusiast, is working as a Software Developer in Bangalore India. She’s a coder by profession and Shuttler by passion. She is involved with Mozilla India Community since 2015. She began her journey as an _FSA_ and went on to become the Founder & Club Lead of Firefox Club of BBDNIIT in 2015 April. Previously, she has contributed to _Firefox OS App Development_, _Localisation_, _QA_, and _SuMo_. She has served on Mozilla's **Featured Add-ons Advisory Board** thrice (July 2017- Dec 2017) (Jan 2018 - June 2018), (Jan 2019 - June 2019). She was also one of the 5 **judges** on the panel in the **Firefox Quantum Extension Challenge**, ran globally from March 15, 2018 - April 15, 2018. She is also a Firefox Add-on Mentor and has published a privacy-themed add-on **PrivateX**. Currently, she is a **Mozilla Tech Speaker** and an **Add-on Content Reviewer** and has also been highlighted as **Friend of Add-ons** in the last quarter of 2018 on Add-ons blog < https://blog.mozilla.org/addons/2018/11/07/friend-of-add-ons-jyotsna-gupta/ >
