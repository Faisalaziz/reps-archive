BioA native of Richmond, Virginia, Sayles holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Social Work from the University of Pittsburgh with his minor in travel and business administration and a large background of strong leadership and community advocacy. Sayles holds a management position in commercial aviation since the year 2000 with a major U.S. air carrier in Chicago, IL.
