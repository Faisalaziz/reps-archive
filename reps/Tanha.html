Tanha Islam

BioGrowing up in the heart of Bangladesh, Dhaka, Tanha started contributing to Mozilla in 2013 in SUMO(Support Mozilla). Became Mozilla Reps and Regional Marketing Ambassador in the same year and started contributing more in event organizing and public speaking along with other contributions. Worked with the SWG(Student Working Group). Initiated and supported to open several Firefox clubs in Bangladesh while being the club lead of her own university Firefox community aka club. Currently serving as a FSA (Firefox Student Ambassador), WoMoz (Women & Mozilla), Webmaker mentor besides the responsibilities of a Reps. Main focus: Get more women in Tech, teach people about Mozilla+open web+web making and work with the students. Besides this, in her spare time, she really enjoys hanging out with friends, trying out different restaurants while learning photography by her own. And, last but not the least, she is a proud Mozillian!
