Marios Isaakidis

Bio{catalyst, internet hacktivist, space storyteller} Born and spent my first years in Thessaloniki, a relaxed city in northern Greece. My story with Mozilla starts back at 2010 and the Mozilla Student Ambassadors program. Currently I'm mostly involved in the Reps program, Mozilla Science (and Software Carpentry), localization, and would like to join the technical evangelism/devrel team. I have represented Mozilla in various places, from Spain to Cyprus and from Sweden to Jordan. Interested in the Internet, as a technology, and as an interconnected system. Studying network architectures and focus on service-centric, software defined networking. In a way, devoted to making the Web a better place.
