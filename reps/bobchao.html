BioStill alive :) not update Reps profile/events a lot though. ### Summary Po-chiang Chao (aka. BobChao) started his Mozillian life as a volunteer since 2002 and then co-founded the community web site [moztw.org](http://moztw.org) in 2004. He is mainly focused on contributor engagement, helping others achieve their goals on Mozilla projects. ### Besides Mozilla Bob was a translator of some technical books, the Development Manager of [Creative Commons Taiwan](http://creativecommons.tw/), and then a User Experience Designer in [wwwinsISOBAR](http://isobar.com.tw).
